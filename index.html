About E-Care

E-care is centered towards achieving Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) goal number three (Good Health and well being).


It provides Health Record Management which provide solution to Hospital Record Mismanagement which has in turn led to loss of several lives.


Rural-Connect focuses on extending good health care to rural dwellers by connecting the rural community health care providers with their senior counterparts at urban health centers closest to them in order to receive guidance in handling emergencies and other critical conditions.
